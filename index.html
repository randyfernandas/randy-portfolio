Piring Nusantara

Piring Nusantara is an interactive Augmented Reality (AR) application that allows users to explore the rich culinary heritage of Indonesia through 3D models of traditional dishes from various regions. Developed using Unity, the app leverages AR technology to deliver an immersive and educational experience. With Piring Nusantara, users can point their devices at specific markers, and detailed 3D models of iconic Indonesian dishes such as rendang, sate, or nasi tumpeng will appear in real-time. Each model is accompanied by information on the dish’s origin, ingredients, and preparation methods, providing a deeper understanding of Indonesia’s culinary heritage.

Bonk Toys

Bonk Toys is an online shop application specifically designed for action figure collectors and enthusiasts. Developed using Flutter (Dart), this app provides a comprehensive platform for browsing and purchasing a wide range of action figures, from superheroes to anime characters, and beyond. The app offers an easy-to-navigate interface that allows users to explore various categories of action figures, view detailed product descriptions, and examine high-resolution images of each figure. Whether you're looking for iconic superhero models or your favorite anime characters, Bonk Toys ensures a smooth shopping experience. The app also features a secure payment gateway, ensuring users can shop with confidence.

Bonk Garage

Bonk Garage is an online shop app designed for diecast model car enthusiasts. Developed using Android Studio with Kotlin, the app offers a streamlined platform for browsing and purchasing a wide range of diecast model cars, from classic cars to modern supercars, catering to collectors of all ages. The app features an elegant and user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to explore the various collections and find their favorite diecast models. Each product page includes detailed information, high-resolution images, and user reviews to assist buyers in making informed decisions. With a secure and efficient checkout system, Bonk Garage ensures a hassle-free shopping experience.

Puff & Poof

Puff & Poof is a dedicated online shop application for plush toy lovers, offering a wide variety of adorable and high-quality stuffed animals. Developed using Android Studio with Kotlin, this app provides a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience, allowing users to browse, select, and purchase their favorite plush toys with ease. The app features a beautifully designed interface that showcases a curated collection of plush toys, ranging from cute animals to fantasy characters, ensuring there is something for everyone. Users can explore product details, view multiple images, and read customer reviews before making a purchase. The secure checkout process guarantees a safe and hassle-free transaction.

Pure Plate

Pure Plate is an online shop application designed for health-conscious individuals seeking nutritious and balanced meals. Developed using Android Studio with Kotlin, the app provides users with a curated selection of healthy meals, each accompanied by detailed nutritional information to help them make informed food choices. Pure Plate offers a smooth and intuitive shopping experience, where users can browse through various meal options, view ingredients, and analyze nutritional values such as calories, protein, fat, and carbohydrate content. Whether you're on a specific diet or just looking for wholesome meals, Pure Plate ensures you find the perfect dish for your lifestyle. The app’s secure payment and fast checkout process ensure a seamless user experience.

Willify

The minimalist layout keeps the focus on the music, while responsive design ensures that the website works smoothly on any device.

Dok Dok

Dok Dok is a telemedicine application prototype designed to provide accessible and affordable healthcare services for underserved communities. The prototype aims to address the challenges faced by individuals who struggle to access medical care due to geographical limitations or financial constraints. By leveraging digital technology, Dok Dok enables users to consult with licensed medical professionals remotely, receive medical advice, and access essential healthcare services from the comfort of their homes.
